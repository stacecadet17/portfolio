A Little About Myself

After graduating from San Jose State University with a bachelor's in Kinesiology, I felt that I was not on the path that would lead me to working in a career that I was truly passionate about. After spending some time doing some soul-searching, I became drawn to the idea of learning to become a software engineer. Thus, began my journey of learning to code. Where, after some introductory self study, I enrolled in an immersive, full-time web development bootcamp at Coding Dojo in San Jose, California. I quickly learned that the challenge of learning the ever evolving technologies of software development, combined with the rewarding sense of satisfaction that comes from using your creativity to develop products for the world we live in, was something I was truly passionate about and could see myself making a career out of for years to come. When I am not developing, I love to nurture my creative side by dancing and teaching dance.
